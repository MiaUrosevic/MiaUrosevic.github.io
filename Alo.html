Alo Yoga is a Los Angeles–based activewear brand founded in 2007, blending athletic performance with fashion-forward designs.

The brand emphasizes sleek, minimalist designs that work for both workouts and casual everyday wear.

Alo targets consumers who value aesthetics and lifestyle branding alongside performance, often collaborating with influencers, athletes, and celebrities.

Financial Performance: Alo Yoga uses a direct-to-consumer business model that supports higher margins and rapid expansion.

Brand Identity: Focused on lifestyle branding and visual appeal, Alo emphasizes clean design and social media presence.

Marketing Strategy: Influencer partnerships and online campaigns strengthen its engagement with customers.
