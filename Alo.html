Alo Yoga is a Los Angeles–based activewear brand founded in 2007. The brand is known for blending athletic performance with fashion-forward designs, making its clothing popular both in workouts and everyday settings.

Alo products are typically priced in the mid-to-high range and emphasize style as much as functionality. The brand focuses on sleek designs, neutral color palettes, and fabrics that transition easily from studio workouts to casual wear.

Alo targets consumers who value aesthetics and lifestyle branding alongside performance. The company has a strong presence on social media and often collaborates with influencers, athletes, and celebrities.

Alo is known for its minimalist aesthetic and clean design approach.

The brand places a strong emphasis on lifestyle branding and visual identity.

Alo Yoga frequently uses digital marketing and influencer partnerships to reach its audience.
